## 🖌 Overview  
Dropshare Advanced is a fast, privacy-respecting file sharing app that uploads your files, screenshots, and screen recordings directly to your own cloud storage and puts the link on your clipboard instantly.

Drag a file onto the menu bar icon and it's uploaded in seconds. No third-party servers involved — your files go directly to your own S3 bucket, Cloudflare R2, SFTP server, or any compatible storage provider.

Screenshot and recording tools are built in. Capture a selection, a window, or your full screen, annotate it if needed, and share it — all in one fluid action without switching apps.

The app supports dozens of cloud storage providers and can be configured to match any hosting setup. Custom domains, expiring links, and password protection give you full control over how your files are shared.

For designers, developers, and anyone who shares files professionally, Dropshare Advanced is the sharing tool that respects your privacy and fits your infrastructure.
